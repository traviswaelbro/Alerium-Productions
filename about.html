BIOGRAPHY

Alerium Productions is a full service digital studio based out of Terre Haute, IN. Chief engineer Aaron Gant specializes in Hip-Hop, Acoustic, and Metal. Although those are the genres predominantly produced at Alerium Productions, we have experience with many other styles of music. Alerium Productions in partnership with catapult digital distribution allows you the opportunity to market your music on popular music sites such as iTunes, Amazon, and Rhapsody. Utilization of software such as Pro Tools 11 and high quality plugins is how Alerium Productions achieves its sound. We have a full suite of plugins from various top brand names such as Native Instruments and Izotope.

If you have any questions or inquiries about studio time contact us at aleriumproductions@gmail.com.
