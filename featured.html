FEATURED ARTISTS

  • Mallory Eilbracht enjoys making music to express herself and simply because it is something she loves. The 22 year old German Studies major enjoys every aspect of art and music. She loves to travel and loves to sing. Mallory's goal is to make music that people enjoy and to inspire people in any way she can. She believes music is a great way to connect with people and would eventually love to write her own songs. Based in the small town of Terre Haute, she is a believer that if you follow your dreams, anything is possible.

  • Chelsea is a Christian artist from Lewis, Indiana hoping to branch out, help others through music, and spread the love of God. She is nineteen years old and plays the guitar and piano. She is a worship leader as well as a youth mentor at her home church in Jasonville, Indiana. Her passion for music started at a young age, performing in school concerts at the age of 8 and teaching herself guitar and piano. God has made Himself known to Chelsea through music and ministry. "All I am is His; I do what I do for Him. I'm not going to push it on people but getting to know God is the best decision I have ever made."

  • Brittany Gibson is a full time student studying exercise science at Indiana State University. She is happily engaged to her college sweetheart, Eli. Together they are raising their beautiful daughter, as well as two dogs, one cat, and one horse. While juggling her busy schedule of work, horse training, and school, Brittany enjoys recording covers of music and is venturing into writing original songs.
